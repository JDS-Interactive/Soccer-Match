⚽ Soccer Match Deck

A live soccer party card game for your phone. Deal a real deck, let each player lock one card into the round, resolve the real match event together, award points, and capture losing cards into each winner’s pile.

🃏 Full 52-card deck
👥 Multi-player rounds
🏆 Capture piles + scoreboard
⏱️ Live countdown timers
⚽ Ends when the real match ends
💾 Saves on your device

Suit Rules

♥ Hearts = goal pressure and finishing.
Goal scored or no goal in the window.

♠ Spades = defense and prevention.
Save, defensive stop, or no shot on target.

♦ Diamonds = momentum and buildup.
Corner, dangerous attack, or possession swing.

♣ Clubs = chaos and disruption.
Foul, yellow card, substitution, or VAR check.
